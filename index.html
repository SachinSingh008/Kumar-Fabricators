About Us

Kumar Fabricators is a leading fabrication company specializing in the design, manufacturing, and installation of a wide range of structures on a contract basis. With a strong emphasis on quality and innovation, we have established ourselves as a reliable partner for clients seeking high-quality fabricated products.

With 20+ years of experience, we ensure quality, precision, and timely delivery for diverse industrial sectors including manufacturing, construction, logistics, and more.

Kumar Fabricators operates a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility equipped with cutting-edge machinery and tools.
